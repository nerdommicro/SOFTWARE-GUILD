Restaurant Description

Nerdom's City buffet features the hottest American dishes in town. Home of the famous deep fried twinkies and desserts. Also at our restaurant you are free to bring in any food item and we will deep fry it for you. Specials and happy hour occurs between the hours of 11AM and 6PM Monday to Friday.
